Conservation Challenges

Wild animals and birds face increasing threats due to human activity and environmental changes. Here are some of the biggest challenges that threaten wildlife around the world.

Deforestation

Massive forest loss destroys animal habitats, leaving many species without food or shelter.

Poaching & Illegal Trade

Pollution

Plastic waste, oil spills, and chemicals pollute oceans and forests, harming both land and marine life.

Climate Change

Rising temperatures and extreme weather destroy ecosystems and force species to migrate or perish.

Habitat Fragmentation

Expanding cities and roads break animal habitats into small patches, isolating populations and reducing survival chances.

Species Extinction

Over 1 million species are at risk of extinction due to human activity, with many already lost forever.
